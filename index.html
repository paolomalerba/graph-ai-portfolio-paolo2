About Me

Hi! I’m Paolo Malerba, an Emergency Medicine physician and current PhD candidate at CRIMEDIM (University of Novara). My professional journey includes clinical work across Lombardy hospitals, coordination roles with Emergency NGO, and peer‑reviewed research on cardiovascular and global health topics.

I’m passionate about leveraging emerging technologies—especially generative AI—to advance healthcare, streamline research, and empower teams. This portfolio will document my progress throughout the Generative AI for Work & Research course.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week, live‑online certification that teaches no‑code AI tools for task automation, data analysis, content creation, and knowledge management.

Next cohort: May 9, 2025  |  Format: Online classroom
Choose one of the weekly 2‑hour workshop times: Fridays 15:00 GMT or Sundays 17:00 GMT.

Course Highlights

  • Automate documents, spreadsheets, and data workflows
  • Organize knowledge and perform literature reviews with AI
  • Create custom chatbots & RAG systems
  • Design AI workflows for task automation across platforms
  • Produce visuals, presentations, and outreach materials with GenAI
  • Capstone project to showcase a real‑world AI solution
